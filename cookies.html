Cookie Policy

At Buddyex, we use cookies to enhance and personalize the user experience on our platform. This cookie policy outlines what cookies are, how we use them, and how users can control their use.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on a user's device when they visit a website. They are used to remember user preferences and improve the user experience.

How we use cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember user preferences and settings
  • Analyze user behavior and improve our platform
  • Provide personalized content and advertising
    • We use both session cookies (which expire when the user closes their browser) and persistent cookies (which remain on the user's device until they expire or are deleted). We may also use third-party cookies (such as Google Analytics) to track user behavior and collect data for analytics purposes.

    Managing cookies

    Users can control the use of cookies through their browser settings. Most browsers allow users to:

  • View and delete cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies
    • However, please note that blocking all cookies may impact the user experience and prevent some features from functioning properly.
